let me present myself...

I am Oussema Hassini, I am 14 and I code. I am a tunisian developer fond of new technologies and open source. I have always enjoyed programming and computers in general, It has always fascinated me and that led me to exploring the amazing word of computer science. My will helped my a lot through my hard journey and I was able to quickly gain knowledge and experience in many fields. I am now proud to say that I am a Java Android developer, a Python and Flask master, a C and C# passionate, an Arduino enthusiast and a web developer embracing new technologies and advancements.

It all started on a friday evening in my local library when I saw that book, "master programming in 1 month" was its title. It was written in french and it really taught me not only the basics of programming but also the building blocks of computer science. I still remember my self trying so hard to understand all of these new terms and concepts. I didn't speak english really good and that time and it was almost impossible to find tutorials in my native language so that book was my only source of information at that time. By the time I finished the book and it was a true challenge, I have gotten my basics and I wanted to learn more and to expand my knowledge. So I moved on to learning game development with Unity and C# and I started discovering forums, StackOverflow and the awesome community of developers on the net. And that my fellow readers is how this awesome journey started and believe me I am still at the beginning.

Favourite projects :

Pox

Pox is the biggest project I have worked on. It's basically a chat app that has location at the core of its functionalities. It lets you share current location, places, paths and also has a special feature to share quick fast messages.

Dummi

Dummi is basically an AI chat bot that learns from the people it talks to. The more users talk it has the better it gets at answering questions and maintaining a conversation. It has quite a small database for the moment but it's growing.

Free time

Free time is a set of python scrips I develop in my free time. It's really only for fun but it has many useful scripts like one that censors curse words in real time on Windows or another one that lets you use your phone as a numpad.

Don't pop it

Don't pop it is a Unity game I have developed in my early days of programming. The game goal is, depending on the mode, either to avoid popping balloons or to pop as much in 30s. It's one of my first serious projects so I had to put it here.
